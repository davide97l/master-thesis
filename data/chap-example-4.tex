\chapter{CITING YOUR REFERENCES}

The template supports BibTex and BibLaTex to cite your references. Below is an example usage for BibTex with \pkg{natbib}:


\section{Sequential numbering}

Under the sequencing numbering system, the default \cs{cite} command is the same as \cs{citep}. The number will be included within the square brackets.

Continual references will be automatically be replaced by a short dash.

% Example citations
\thusetup{
  cite-style = super,
}
\begin{tabular}{l@{\quad$\Rightarrow$\quad}l}
  \verb|\cite{zhangkun1994}|               & \cite{zhangkun1994}               \\
  \verb|\citet{zhangkun1994}|              & \citet{zhangkun1994}              \\
  \verb|\citep{zhangkun1994}|              & \citep{zhangkun1994}              \\
  \verb|\cite[42]{zhangkun1994}|           & \cite[42]{zhangkun1994}           \\
  \verb|\cite{zhangkun1994,zhukezhen1973}| & \cite{zhangkun1994,zhukezhen1973} \\
\end{tabular}

\section{Author with year}

Citing with authors' name and year, \cs{cite} and \cs{citet} are equivalent.

\thusetup{
  cite-style = author-year,
}
\begin{tabular}{l@{\quad$\Rightarrow$\quad}l}
  \verb|\cite{zhangkun1994}|                & \cite{zhangkun1994}                \\
  \verb|\citet{zhangkun1994}|               & \citet{zhangkun1994}               \\
  \verb|\citep{zhangkun1994}|               & \citep{zhangkun1994}               \\
  \verb|\cite[42]{zhangkun1994}|            & \cite[42]{zhangkun1994}            \\
  \verb|\citep{zhangkun1994,zhukezhen1973}| & \citep{zhangkun1994,zhukezhen1973} \\
\end{tabular}
